Our Partners

Hospitals, first responders, universities, housing organizations, and community groups stand with WGMC so neighbors can access free healthcare, shelter, and hope.

Refer a partner Volunteer with WGMC
Powered by collaboration

What partners make possible

Our network of partners fuels free medical and behavioral healthcare, specialty referrals, housing navigation, outreach, and wraparound support for adults across Will and Grundy Counties.

  • 🩺Clinical volunteers, diagnostics, and specialty consults that keep care free for patients.
  • 🏡Housing outreach, case management, and placements that move neighbors toward stability.
  • 🤝Education, public safety, and civic partners who connect patients to resources that help them thrive.

About WGMC

WGMC is a free care organization providing healthcare and social services in Will & Grundy Counties. Free medical and behavioral healthcare is provided to uninsured and underinsured adults, and free housing support and case management is provided to residents who are homeless or housing insecure.
