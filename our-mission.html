Our Mission

WGMC creates the standard for comprehensive health and social care — serving uninsured and underinsured adults across Will & Grundy Counties.

M

Our Mission

Create the standard for comprehensive health and social care.

Since 1988, WGMC has served as the only free clinic providing wraparound healthcare services to uninsured and under-insured adults in Will and Grundy Counties. Since 2020, WGMC has evolved to also include homelessness support and care coordination services, designing community programs with the belief that all people deserve equal opportunities to health and wellness.

V

Our Vision

Create healthy and vibrant communities through a holistic approach to compassionate care.

Our Core Values

1

Collective Care

We believe that health and healing thrive through partnership, collaboration, and inclusion. By working together — patients, providers, and community partners — we build trust and ensure compassionate, holistic, and equitable care for every individual we serve.

2

Courageous Compassion

We lead with honesty, empathy, and a growth mindset. Through transparency and courage, we face challenges openly, learn from experience, and continuously strengthen the quality of care and support we provide to our patients and one another.

3

Innovating Together

We know that lasting impact comes from teamwork, creativity, and community connection. By combining our collective strengths and fresh ideas, we find new ways to advance health, inspire hope, and create meaningful change.

About WGMC

WGMC is a free care organization providing healthcare and social services in Will & Grundy Counties. Free medical and behavioral healthcare is provided to uninsured and underinsured adults, and free housing support and case management is provided to residents who are homeless or housing insecure.
